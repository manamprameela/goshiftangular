GOSHIFT

Shift work is common for almost any business that operates for 8 hours a day or longer. The reason for this is that employers don't have the financial resources or they're unwilling to pay employees overtime. As a result, they'll split the workload into shifts that keep their workers below 40 hours a week. This reduces overhead, as well as keeping employees alert and sharp.
